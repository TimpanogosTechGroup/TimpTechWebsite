Our Mission Statement

Why We Started Timpanogos Tech

Isaac Draper
Ben Brenkman

Here at Timpanogos Tech we have two main goals. First, to prepare ourselves for the workforce by starting now and second, to create real-world products for what we are passionate about.


Employers want the very best; they expect employees to be creative, think out of the box, work hard, and be efficient. Fitting in with a company requires a different set of skills than taking tests, doing homework and taking good notes during lectures.


These are skills that need to be developed outside of class and students who are passionate, willing to learn, and work hard will develop them the most.


Our philosophy is: Why wait?


We want to develop these skills now. If we do, we will be more prepared for a fast-paced work environment. At Timpanogos Tech, you can work with a team to develop a real-world product from start to finish.


This provides the opportunity to work as a team, develop skills, and showcase talent as a tangible product. The goal for every team is create something they are passionate about. If you are passionate about something, you will go above and beyond what is required.


It is difficult to get invested in a classroom project, since it has no real purpose besides education. Timpanogos Tech teams work on real world projects that go beyond the classroom. From laser tag systems to machine learning programming, what matters is that the team behind the project is committed to completion..
